Supplies

  • 1 roll -packing tape
  • 1-2 roll - double sided tape
  • Scissors
  • Prints

Making the Book

Step 1

Order prints from photo printing centre (ex. London Drugs), with special comment of having the photos "Fit In".

Step 2

Using packing tape, connect Photos together hiding the tape by attaching the tape to the back side of the photos. Continue this taping till "Or a better break" page.

Step 3

Using double sided tape, starting on the back side of, "Or a better break," place two lines of tape and continue placing photos the end of the book.

Step 4

Using packing tape, join cover together and double sided tape the introduction poem on backside.

Step 5

Using packing tape attach cover to main page. Fold cover and crease top half.
